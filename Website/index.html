About the work.

01 / 04
Syed Talib Haider STH Delhi · 2026

I'm Haider, a Delhi-based editor and content strategist who spent five years in fast-paced digital sports newsrooms. Started as a writer at Premier League News Now, moved into editing, then content management. Today I lead editorial operations at Pro Football & Sports Network.

The work sits at the meeting point of audience psychology, SEO, and AI-assisted production. Fluent in GA4, Google Discover behaviour, E-E-A-T, and the daily rhythm of a newsroom that lives on live traffic dashboards. I track what's working in real time, double down on it, and bin what isn't.

Traditional journalism stays cautious about AI for good reason. I keep a no-AI-writing line on the words themselves. The workflow around the words is a different question: ideation, competitor scans, trend extraction, fact-check passes. That's where Claude Sonnet and Gemini Pro earn their keep without compromising originality or E-E-A-T.

A stint as an Affiliate Content Producer with The Sporting News taught me to build frameworks that ranked and converted, using the same audience psychology that fuels good brand strategy. That blend of newsroom speed plus brand thinking is the lens I bring to everything now.
